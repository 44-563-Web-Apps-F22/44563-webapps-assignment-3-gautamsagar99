Colorado Railroad Museum

Bringing Colorado railroad history to life year-round. Stroll the Museum's 15-acre railyard featuring more than 100 historic railcars. Also, tour exhibit galleries, a renowned library, roundhouse restoration facility and working turntable. Minutes from downtown Denver.

Directions from Kansas to Museum

The closet airport is Kansas International Airport

  1. After coming out from the airport walk towards NW Roanridge road.
    1. Walk 100 meters towards north.
    2. you can find a sign board on the right side which is attached to a road, which will directly take you to the Museum.
